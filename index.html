SBMbeats

In 2020, on behalf of SBM LABEL, I created a special platform for sharing beats by producers for SBM artists. I have developed the most effective way of segregating beats, unlike adding bits on platforms such as Youtube, Soundcloud, I have enabled rappers to search for a beat at the right pitch, bpm etc. using filters. In addition, I decided to make the page so that all operations were performed as simply as possible and required a few clicks, and the interface was as simple as possible. For the convenience of using the website, I added saving to the Local Storage informations what part of website we used during the last session.

songrator

In 2019, I wrote a platform for transferring projects between producers and rappers. The main purpose of the platform was to safely send the unpublished song and the ability to listen to it. In addition, I focused on the simplicity of the site, so adding a song and sending it took a few seconds and only a two mouse clicks. The website, despite a narrow audience, generated monthly traffic of 1500+ unique users, of which over 30% were regular users of the platform. Over 40gb of music was added to the site in 5 months.
